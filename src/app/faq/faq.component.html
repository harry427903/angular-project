FAQ

Frequently asked questions about FundFusion

What is FundFusion?

FundFusion is a comprehensive digital banking platform that provides a range of financial services, including account management, fund transfers, loan applications, and more.

How does billing work for FundFusion?

FundFusion offers various billing options, such as monthly and annual subscription plans, as well as pay-as-you-go pricing for certain services. Payments can be conveniently made through your linked bank account.

Can I get a refund for my subscription with FundFusion?

FundFusion offers a 30-day money-back guarantee for most subscription plans. If you are not satisfied within the first 30 days, you can request a full refund. Subscriptions active for more than 30 days may be reviewed on a case-by-case basis.

What is the minimum balance requirement for FundFusion accounts?

FundFusion accounts do not have a minimum balance requirement, making it accessible to a wide range of users, including those who want to start with a small balance.

What types of accounts can I open with FundFusion?

FundFusion offers a variety of accounts, including savings accounts, checking accounts, investment accounts, and more. You can choose the one that best suits your financial goals.

Can I access FundFusion from mobile devices?

Absolutely! FundFusion is designed to be mobile-responsive. You can access your accounts, perform transactions, and manage your finances conveniently from your smartphone or tablet.
